About Us

Oxyfoss Private Limited is a technology solutions provider established in 2016, delivering comprehensive business and IT solutions to organizations across Kerala. We specialize in computer hardware, technical support, office automation solutions, networking, surveillance systems, and technology infrastructure services. Since our inception, we have been committed to helping businesses, institutions, and organizations adopt reliable and cost-effective technology solutions. Our expertise extends beyond product supply to consultation, deployment, maintenance, and after-sales support, ensuring that clients receive complete end-to-end assistance for their technology requirements. With years of industry experience, Oxyfoss has built a reputation for reliability, consistency, and customer-focused service. We have supported customers from diverse sectors including government organizations, hospitality, banking, education, healthcare, and private enterprises, providing solutions tailored to their operational needs. Our portfolio includes computers and peripherals, networking equipment, security and surveillance systems, information kiosks, office automation products, air-conditioning solutions, access points, printers, and a wide range of technology accessories from globally recognized brands. At Oxyfoss, we believe that technology should simplify operations, improve efficiency, and support growth. By combining quality products, technical expertise, and responsive customer support, we continue to help organizations embrace technology with confidence.
